Interview with Ruchit

The questions were asked by Ferdan.

Why do you want to be a designer?

I want to be a designer because I like design, art and I would like to improve further so that I can become a graphic designer.

What makes you nervous about becoming a designer?

Being good enough because graphic design is a competitive work field.

What excites you about design?

Just being able to create things using technology and our creativity.

What are your strengths? What are some areas of opportunity to work on?

My strengths are actually working on the adobe creative cloud apps and something I would like to work on is my design ability to create new ideas.

What aspects of graphic design do you gravitate towards?

Creating logos and cool designs.

What are the roles and responsibilities of a graphic designer in society?

Our roles and responsibilities of being graphic designers is basically the fact that we are in charge of potentially making advertisements of businesses which will help people get to know the companies product and attract other customers.

When and where are you at your most creative/productive?

After work, at home and it's a good environment to get ideas to move forward with my projects.
